About Us

Vaishya Youth Association , Kochi is a youth organisation which was founded in 1968 for building the enthusiastic, cultured, socialistic youth from and for the konkani speaking vaishya community as well as for the society. In 2018 the organisation celebrated it’s golden jubilee in the divine presence of vaishya guru SRI SRI VAMANASRAM SWAMIJI.

The Vaishya Youth Association Kochi was the first organisation which was formed for our community in kerala. In 1971 T G Rathnakar , trustee of Sreemadh janardhana Devaswam allotted the building located in the althara bagavathy temple compound for the Association ,thus the first office was started in that building . in 1970 ‘s there was no facilities or encouragement for the students in the community for their studies. At that time VYA encouraged students by giving cash awards for the students who passed SSLC. In 1974 membership was given for all youth in the community who were of age 18. At that time the committee was focused mainly on the uplift of the students in their education as well as their passion. The fund scholarships were raised by conducting film fest and drama fest.

In 1985 the bylaw was created which filtered the member age to 18-35 . For the uplift of the youth with certain passion and interest in the arts and sports association started an annual arts and sports meet which was conducted from Vijayadhashami to Diwali. By the 3rd decade of youth’s history the office was changed to the building in the compound of Sree janardhana temple as a part of the old building renovation .But after the renovation the building in althara bagavathy temple compound was’nt allowed for the association.

On the alumni program conducted by Golden jubilee committee which had delegates from Sree Janardhana Devaswom , Alumni members requested for the reallocation of the office in the very first location. Thus the Golden jubilee committee decided to build the first floor for the office as a Golden Jubilee memorial with the permission of the trustees.
