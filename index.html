About Us

Vaishya Youth Association,kochi which was initially started in 1968 and officially registered in 1969. On 2018 VYA has celebrated it's 50th year with a grand function which was inaugrated by vaishya guru Sri Sri Vamanshrama Swamiji . VYA is the youth wing of Vaishya Samaj which contains the history of the community, its people, their social life, tradition, culture, arts, education, information regarding their temples, other institutions, members who made achievements in various fields like education, arts, culture, and literature as well as related with the civic bodies.
